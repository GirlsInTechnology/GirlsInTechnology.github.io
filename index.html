About Us

Girls in Technology (GIT) is a non-profit focused on the engagement and awareness of girls and women who are passionate about technology. Our aim is to enhance the growth of innovative women who are entering into the high-tech industry and building successful startups.

Girls in Technology is a common platform dedicated to girls where students, professionals and beginners of technology background come together and share.The main goal of Girls In Technology is focussed on bringing/producing the skillset required for girls/women that will help them come across their technology oriented career.Withi

Well to be honest, I never thought I would be joining the path of technology, as I intended to build up my career in medical field. But my bad I couldn’t get an entrance to medical school because of some delicate reasons. After my failure to involve in medical sector, I was confused, agonized, and scared to select my next career option. But however after so much of consults and suggestion I came to choose the field of technology. When I was advised to join IT by my beloved people I was not so ready to confront this sector. Once I got involved in it I found it interesting as well as challenging and tough at the same time. As I mentioned I was scared to join IT sector not only because I was not prepared for that but also because I counted myself among the below averages in mathematics and computer knowledge. This fear of mine did not served me well. At the very beginning when I started learning things about technology I felt like if I was doomed. My fear started to turn into my anger and then agony, I was not able to take things into my mind, and I felt as if I choose the wrong path. But patience is something each and every one should have, which I could manage to have during my starter period. With my consistent hard work, full time dedication and will power to achieve something in IT field I managed to learn programing. Though I started learning it was not so easy, many time I pushed myself into dilemma of leaving, but fortunately I didn’t. Now I am good at my works and love programming, finally I made a cut after years of dedication and hard work. And another proud moment will be the one when I will be counted as one among the few girls involved in technology field in our country. With the enrollment, what I personally feel of technology, is the only arena where girls can compete with boys and prove their skills, strength and ability. Not only this, women with technology skills are more likely to possess different personality. With success queuing behind us we can influence many other to join this sector as this might be the only field which will keep growing and growing. Participation:  I have participated and enjoyed myself in motivational program, organized by Girls in Technology Nepal, to increase the enrollment of girls in technology. I felt great towards GIT as they gave me the chance to involve in various programs as member part of technology.  And beside that I have participated and personally involved in many programs organized by Pride Nepal and many other organizations, among those programs I individually assisted my team during relief program organized by them after the massive earthquake that hit Nepal on 24th April 2015.
